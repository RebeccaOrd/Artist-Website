About

Lives and works in Glasgow, Scotland

I work across painting, printmaking, and sculpture to explore themes of transformation, identity, and belonging. By using found and repurposed materials, I weave together ancient stories and contemporary narratives to reveal the intersections of myth, memory, and our connection to the land and ourselves. Through ritualistic processes, I delve into the personal and collective experiences of women, giving voice to our forgotten histories.
